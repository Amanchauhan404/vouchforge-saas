# VouchForge AI Name Check

The working product name is **VouchForge AI**. It is a stronger starting point than the earlier TrustStream AI concept because the latter has visible collision risk with existing cybersecurity and monitoring products.

## What this check means

- This repository uses the VouchForge AI name in product copy only.
- A domain/RDAP result or web search is not trademark clearance.
- Do not buy a large advertising campaign, file a company name, or claim exclusive rights based only on this document.

## Before public launch

1. Search the [USPTO Trademark Search](https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/search) for `VouchForge`, `Vouch Forge`, and close phonetic variants in relevant classes.
2. Search the [WIPO Global Brand Database](https://branddb.wipo.int/en/quicksearch) for international conflicts.
3. Search the Indian Trade Marks Registry if India is a target market.
4. Check domain availability and social handles on the day you register them.
5. Ask a trademark professional for clearance in every market where you will sell. File the mark before making substantial brand investments when practical.

## Naming guardrails

- Use `VouchForge AI` as the product name until clearance is complete.
- Avoid describing the name as registered, protected, or exclusive before filing and acceptance.
- Keep a dated record of the searches, results, and legal advice you receive.
